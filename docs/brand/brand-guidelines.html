MEASURE. UNDERSTAND. IMPROVE.

TraceYield is a closed-loop discipline for managing the cost and efficacy of LLM interactions — and the reference tool that makes it possible for engineering teams.

Brand idea

Tokens are the meter,
not the mission.

TraceYield brings the discipline of operations to LLM usage. We measure in tokens, but we optimize for value per token. We turn data into insight and insight into action — closing the loop of describe, diagnose, predict, prescribe, remediate so teams get steadily cheaper and more effective.

Data-driven

Measure what matters — token by token.

Insightful

Surface the why, not just the what.

Actionable

Recommend the next best change.

Closed-loop

Act, measure, learn, repeat.

Private by default

Your data stays on your machine.

The logo

The mark combines a hexagon (structure, systems) with a bar / token motif (measurement), inside a gradient path that implies a circular flow — the closed loop.
